Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 12, 2025

Introduction

Arc Helper ("we", "our", or "app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains our data practices.

Privacy First: Arc Helper does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Your privacy is fully protected.

Data Collection

Arc Helper does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information. Specifically, the app:

  • Does not require account creation
  • Does not collect names, emails, or contact information
  • Does not access your device's camera, microphone, or location
  • Does not track your usage or behavior
  • Stores no data on external servers

Local Data Storage

All app data is stored exclusively locally on your device. This data includes only:

  • App preferences and settings
  • No personal or identifying information

Third-Party Services

Arc Helper uses one third-party service for monetization:

  • Google AdMob: We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect certain non-personal data for ad serving, including:
    • Device advertising ID
    • IP address
    • Device information
    • Ad interaction data

    For AdMob's full privacy policy, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Children's Privacy

Arc Helper does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is generally suitable for all ages and requires no personal information to use.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page and/or within the app with an updated "Last Updated" date.
